Refund Policy

At Flower Bouquets, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy, please review the following refund policy.

Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, you must request it within 14 days of your purchase and provide proof of purchase.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Exchanges

If you received a damaged or defective item, we are happy to exchange it for a new one.

Non-Refundable Items

Certain perishable goods such as custom flower arrangements cannot be returned.
