Shipping Policy

Flower Bouquets offers fast and reliable shipping to ensure that your flowers arrive fresh and on time. Please review our shipping policy for more details.

Shipping Methods

We offer standard and expedited shipping options at checkout. Delivery times may vary depending on your location.

Shipping Fees

Shipping fees are calculated based on your location and the size of your order. These fees will be displayed at checkout.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders placed over the weekend or on holidays will be processed the next business day.

International Shipping

We currently only ship to addresses within the United States. We do not offer international shipping at this time.
